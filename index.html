About neuronova

Neuronova is the premier hackathon of the AIML Department at BMS Institute of Technology and Management (BMSIT), Bangalore, conducted under the auspices of the AR/VR Club. This event is exclusively for the AIML students of BMSIT.

The hackathon features two exciting tracks for participants to choose from:

  • Artificial Intelligence (AI)
  • Augmented Reality / Virtual Reality (AR/VR)

Specific problem statements for each track will be disclosed 24 hours prior to the start of the event, giving teams ample time to strategize.

FAQ

  • Who can participate?
    Participation is exclusive to AIML students of BMS Institute of Technology and Management.
  • Is there a registration fee?
    No, registration is completely free!
  • What is the team size?
    Teams should consist of 3 to 4 members.
  • What should participants bring?
    Participants are required to bring their own laptops, chargers, and any other necessary accessories.
  • Will food and beverages be provided?
    Yes, snacks, meals (lunch, dinner), and beverages will be provided throughout the event.
  • What are the hackathon tracks?
    There are two tracks: Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Augmented Reality / Virtual Reality (AR/VR). Problem statements will be released 24 hours before the event.
